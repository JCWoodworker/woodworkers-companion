PRIVACY POLICY FOR THE WOODWORKER'S COMPANION

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

OVERVIEW
The Woodworker's Companion is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT
The app allows you to create and store project information, inventory data, client details, and quotes. All data is stored locally on your device only.

DATA STORAGE
- All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers
- No cloud backup or synchronization
- Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it

HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
Your data is used exclusively for app functionality: displaying your information, performing calculations, and generating reports. We do NOT share, sell, or transmit your data.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
The app uses Expo framework. No other third-party services are used. No analytics, advertising, or tracking.

PERMISSIONS
- CAMERA (optional): Only used if you choose to attach photos to inventory items. Feature is disabled by default. Photos stay on your device.
- READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save your project and inventory data locally
- VIBRATE: For haptic feedback (can be disabled in settings)
- INTERNET: Required by app framework (no data transmitted to servers)

DATA RETENTION
Data is stored on your device indefinitely until you delete it or uninstall the app.

YOUR RIGHTS
You have complete control over your data. You can view, edit, delete, and export all data within the app.

DATA DELETION
Delete individual items in the app, clear app data in device settings, or uninstall the app.

CONTACT
Email: [Your email address]

COMPLIANCE
This app complies with Google Play Store policies, COPPA, GDPR, and CCPA.

SUMMARY
- What we collect: Project, inventory, and client data you enter
- Where it's stored: Locally on your device only
- Who has access: Only you
- What we do with it: Nothing. It never leaves your device.
