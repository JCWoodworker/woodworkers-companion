# Privacy Policy for The Woodworker's Companion

**Last Updated**: November 8, 2025

## Overview

The Woodworker's Companion ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide

The Woodworker's Companion allows you to create and store the following types of information:

- **Projects**: Project names, descriptions, status, dates, estimated costs, client associations, tasks, time entries, and expenses
- **Inventory**: Lumber species and quantities, tool information, consumable supplies, hardware items, and associated details (costs, locations, notes)
- **Clients**: Client names, contact information (email, phone), addresses, and notes
- **Documents**: Quotes with item descriptions, quantities, and pricing
- **Settings**: Application preferences and customization options

### Automatically Collected Data

We do **NOT** collect:
- No analytics or tracking data
- No usage statistics
- No device identifiers
- No location data
- No personal identifiable information beyond what you enter

## How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage Only

**All data is stored locally on your device** using:
- Android: AsyncStorage (local device storage)
- iOS: AsyncStorage (local device storage)

**Important**: 
- No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers
- No cloud backup or synchronization (unless you manually export data)
- Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it

### Data Security

- All data is stored in your device's secure storage
- Data is protected by your device's security features (lock screen, encryption)
- Only The Woodworker's Companion app can access this data
- Uninstalling the app will delete all stored data

## How We Use Your Data

The data you enter is used **exclusively** for:

1. **Displaying your information** within the app
2. **Performing calculations** (board feet, pricing, wood movement, etc.)
3. **Generating reports and quotes** for your woodworking projects
4. **Organizing and managing** your inventory and projects

We do **NOT**:
- Share your data with anyone
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Transmit your data to external servers
- Profile or track you

## Data You Share

The Woodworker's Companion allows you to:

- **Export data**: You can export your inventory or project data (when this feature is enabled)
- **Share quotes**: You can share generated quotes via your device's sharing features (email, messaging, etc.)

When you share data using these features:
- You control who receives it
- Data is shared through your device's standard sharing mechanisms
- We do not receive or store any shared data

## Third-Party Services

### Expo Framework

The app is built using Expo, a React Native framework. Expo may collect:
- Error logs and crash reports (if you consent in your device settings)
- Basic app performance metrics

For Expo's privacy practices, see: https://expo.dev/privacy

### No Other Third Parties

We do **NOT** use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage providers
- Payment processors
- Email marketing services

## Permissions

The Woodworker's Companion may request the following Android/iOS permissions:

### Current Permissions:
- **Storage/Files**: To save and load your project and inventory data locally on your device

### Future Permissions (if features are enabled):
- **Camera**: To attach photos to inventory items or projects (optional, only if you use photo features)
- **Photo Library**: To select existing photos from your device (optional)

**Important**: All permissions are optional and only requested when you use specific features. The core functionality works without any special permissions.

## Children's Privacy

The Woodworker's Companion is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Data Retention

- Data is stored on your device **indefinitely** until you:
  - Delete individual items within the app
  - Clear app data through your device settings
  - Uninstall the app

- **Uninstalling the app** will permanently delete all data stored by The Woodworker's Companion
- We have no ability to recover data after uninstallation as we don't store backups

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### You Can:
- ✅ **View** all your data within the app
- ✅ **Edit** any information you've entered
- ✅ **Delete** individual items or all data
- ✅ **Export** your data (when enabled in settings)
- ✅ **Control** which features and fields are visible (Hobbyist/Professional/Custom modes)

### Data Deletion:
- **Within App**: Delete individual projects, inventory items, clients, or quotes
- **Complete Deletion**: Clear app data in device settings or uninstall the app

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Breaches

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers:
- There is no risk of server-side data breaches
- Your data security depends on your device security
- We recommend using device lock screens and encryption

## International Users

The Woodworker's Companion stores all data locally on your device. No data is transferred internationally because no data leaves your device.
