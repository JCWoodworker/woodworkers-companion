PRIVACY POLICY

The Woodworker's Companion stores all data locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. You create projects, inventory records, client information, and quotes - all of which stay on your device. 

CAMERA PERMISSION: Optional. Only requested if you choose to attach photos to inventory items (disabled by default). Photos stay on your device and are never uploaded.

OTHER PERMISSIONS: Storage (to save your data locally), Vibrate (haptic feedback), Internet (required by app framework - no data transmitted).

We use no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party data collection services. Uninstalling the app deletes all data. You have complete control over your information.
